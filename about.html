Our team

Hello, I'm Denali Elsley, and I'm the owner of bay area pet boarding. I have been fostering and taking care of pets for over 5 years and have encountered many types of pets, from dogs and cats to fish. With the help of my family, I founded Bay area pet boarding, so that even though I don't have a pet of my own, I can still take care of and have fun with them. I look forward to meeting you and your furry friend!

Our mission

We create a safe and welcoming environment for all our animals.

Our values

  1. Safety: We aim to make sure that all our pets are cared for in a supervised environment.
  2. Personalized care: We understand that all pets have their own needs, and aim to tailor their experience to their needs.
  3. Joy: We nurture not just the physical but the emotional health of our animals.
