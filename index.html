Our services

See when your pet can stay

Some love the company of other animals, while others feel safest in their own space. That’s why we take the time to learn about your pet’s personality, preferences, and needs before their stay. When you schedule boarding, we’ll let you know about any other pets we’ll have during that time so we can ensure the best environment for your furry friend, whether that means group play, one-on-one attention, or a quieter space all their own. Your pet’s comfort and safety always come first.

The gift of a cozy stay

At Bay Area Pet Boarding, we celebrate the unique spirit of every companion, from the tiniest paws to the grandest wings. Our thoughtfully crafted pricing embraces all creatures, honoring their size and special needs with gentle care. To make your journey easier, we offer the grace of provisional bookings—so you can plan your pet’s stay with calm and confidence, knowing their comfort is our heartfelt promise.
